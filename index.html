Postpartum Myths and Facts

Myth: You Should Bounce Back Immediately

Fact: Postpartum recovery takes time. Every mother's body is different, and there is no set timeline for recovery.

Myth: Postpartum Depression Only Happens Right After Birth

Fact: Postpartum depression can occur any time in the first year after childbirth. It’s important to seek support if you're struggling with your emotions.

Myth: Breastfeeding Will Help You Lose Weight Quickly

Fact: While breastfeeding burns calories, it doesn't guarantee immediate weight loss. Every body responds differently postpartum.

Myth: Asking for Help Means You’re Failing as a Mother

Fact: Asking for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. Postpartum recovery is a team effort, and there is no shame in seeking support.
